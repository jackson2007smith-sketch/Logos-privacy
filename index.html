Privacy Policy

Effective June 25, 2026

Logos is a private reading, reflection, and habit-building app. This policy explains what information the current version collects, why it is collected, and the choices available to you.

1. About Logos

Logos lets users track reading sessions, save books and page bookmarks, record passages and personal reflections, mark favourites, and view reading progress.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: your email address, account identifier, and the display name you provide.
  • Reading information: book titles, authors, page bookmarks, reading-session start and end times, and session duration.
  • Content you choose to save: quotes, passages, page numbers, reflections, and favourite status.
  • Progress information: reading totals, insight count, XP, and level. These values are calculated from your activity in Logos.

3. How information is collected

Most information is provided directly by you when you create an account, add a book, run a reading timer, or save an insight. Progress information is generated automatically from the reading sessions and insights you choose to record.

4. How we use information

Logos uses collected information to:

  • Create, authenticate, and maintain your account.
  • Synchronize your library and save reading sessions and reflections.
  • Restore your reading progress and calculate XP and levels.
  • Operate account security and deletion features.
  • Maintain and troubleshoot the service.

Logos does not use your reading content to advertise to you or build an advertising profile.

5. Storage and service providers

Account and reading data is stored using Supabase, which provides authentication and database infrastructure for Logos. Supabase processes this information on behalf of Logos to provide those services.

Your authenticated session is stored locally on your device so you can remain signed in. While a reading timer is active, its selected book and start time are also stored locally so the timer can recover if the app closes.

6. Information Logos does not collect

The current version of Logos does not include advertising or third-party analytics and does not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Logos does not request access to your location, contacts, camera, microphone, photos, calendar, Bluetooth, or advertising identifier.

7. Sharing and sale of information

Logos does not sell your personal information and does not share it for third-party advertising.

Information may be disclosed when needed for the service provider described above, when required by law, or when reasonably necessary to protect users, Logos, or the security of the service.

8. Retention and deletion

Account and reading data is retained while your Logos account remains active and as needed to provide the app.

You can delete individual insights or books in the Library. Removing a book also removes its associated passages, insights, and reflections.

You can permanently delete your account from the Profile screen. Account deletion removes your authentication account and associated Logos data from the active service, subject to limited backup retention, legal obligations, and technical processing time.

9. Security

Logos uses authenticated access and database row-level security so users can access only their own records. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

10. Your choices and rights

You may choose not to save optional information such as an author, page number, bookmark, or reflection. You may remove saved content or permanently delete your account from within the app.

Depending on where you live, you may also have legal rights to access, correct, export, restrict, or delete personal information.

11. Children's privacy

Logos is not directed to children under the minimum age required to consent to online services in their jurisdiction. If you believe a child has provided personal information without appropriate consent, please contact us.

12. International processing

Information may be processed in countries other than the country where you live. Where required, appropriate safeguards will be used for international transfers.

13. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when Logos changes its features, service providers, or data practices. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when material changes are published.

14. Contact

For privacy questions, requests, or concerns, email Logosreadingapp@gmail.com.
